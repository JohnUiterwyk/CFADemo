Planning For Belleville

A planning team is making decisions about zoning for an area which has opened up for residential development. Belleville is a small valley which is very rural and attractive, however there are serious planning concerns about bushfire risk from the surrounding state forest and there is also a flooding risk in some areas near the river. The planning team must balance these concerns to get the right mix of disaster prevention and residential opportunities.

There are three participants in the planning team. One is concerned with fire issues, another with water management issues, and the third is concerned to make sure that the local council will be financially viable.
